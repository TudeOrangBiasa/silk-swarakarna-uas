# SILK-Swarakarna

Domain glossary for the Sistem Informasi Layanan Klinik Swarakarna — a web app that digitises medical-record entry and examination scheduling for an ENT (THT) clinic specialising in hearing and balance diagnostics.

## Language

**Klinik Swarakarna**:
An outpatient ENT clinic with a focus on hearing and balance disorders. Houses the Audiometri, OAE, and BERA test suites.
_Avoid_: "Klinik THT" (acceptable colloquial alias; Klinik Swarakarna is the canonical name in records), "rumah sakit" (this is not a hospital — no inpatient services).

**THT (Telinga Hidung Tenggorokan)**:
The medical specialisation. All **Dokter** entries in this system are THT specialists.
_Avoid_: "ENT" in user-facing strings; "THT" is the only term surfaced in Bahasa Indonesia.

**Pasien**:
A person who has registered at the clinic and is assigned a **No Rekam Medis**. A **Pasien** may have zero or many **Pemeriksaan**.
_Avoid_: "patient" (use Indonesian), "klien", "customer".

**No Rekam Medis (RM-)**:
A unique identifier of the form `RM-XXX` assigned to each **Pasien** at registration. Auto-generated by the system, never user-supplied.
_Avoid_: "ID Pasien" (conflicts with the database column `id_pasien`; "No Rekam Medis" is the user-facing label).

**Dokter**:
A licensed THT specialist who performs **Pemeriksaan**. Identified by **No Izin Praktik** (practice licence number).
_Avoid_: "dokter umum" (system explicitly tracks specialists only), "practitioner".

**Layanan**:
A billable examination type offered by the clinic. Examples: Audiometri, OAE (Otoacoustic Emission), BERA (Brainstem Evoked Response Audiometry). Each has a fixed **Biaya** (price in IDR).
_Avoid_: "tindakan" (refers to clinical procedures, not billable items), "paket layanan" (no bundled services in v1), "obat" (the system has no medication tracking).

**Pemeriksaan**:
A scheduled clinical encounter that links one **Pasien** to one **Dokter** performing one **Layanan** on a specific date. The transactional unit of the system.
_Avoid_: "transaksi" (acceptable as a low-level alias, but "Pemeriksaan" is the user-facing canonical), "kunjungan" (implies walk-in, but most are pre-scheduled), "order".

**No Transaksi (TRX-)**:
A unique identifier of the form `TRX-YYYYNNN` assigned to each **Pemeriksaan**. The `YYYY` segment is the calendar year; the sequence resets at year boundary. Auto-generated, never user-supplied.
_Avoid_: "ID Pemeriksaan" (conflicts with database column `id_periksa`; "No Transaksi" is the user-facing label).

**Status Pemeriksaan**:
The lifecycle state of a **Pemeriksaan**. Three values, in canonical order: `Menunggu` → `Sedang Diperiksa` → `Selesai`. Admin may skip steps or revert `Sedang Diperiksa` → `Menunggu`. Once `Selesai`, a **Pemeriksaan** is historis (immutable status).
_Avoid_: "status" alone (ambiguous; always qualify with the noun), "state".

**Keluhan**:
Free-text chief complaint recorded at the time of **Pemeriksaan** registration. Required field.
_Avoid_: "catatan" (broader scope; used elsewhere in potential future modules), "diagnosa" (system does not record diagnosis in v1 — only complaint).

**Biaya**:
The price in IDR (rupiah, integer) of a single **Layanan**. Stored as integer to avoid floating-point rounding in financial reports.
_Avoid_: "harga" (used informally; "Biaya" is the formal term on receipts and reports), "tarif".

## Example dialogue

> **Dev**: "Budi daftar sebagai Pasien, dapat RM-042. Tiga hari kemudian dia dijadwalkan untuk Pemeriksaan Audiometri sama Dr. Sari, status awal Menunggu. Setelah dijalankan status jadi Sedang Diperiksa, setelah laporan jadi status Selesai. Kode TRX-2026007 dibuat saat pendaftaran."
>
> **Domain expert**: "Betul. Tapi catat: sekali Selesai, kode TRX tidak boleh dihapus meskipun ada koreksi administratif — kita butuh trail untuk audit."
>
> **Dev**: "Artinya `delete` di tabel `pemeriksaan` harusnya dicegah kalau status = Selesai?"
>
> **Domain expert**: "Iya. Atau tampilkan warning keras. Riwayat medis itu immutable once finalised."
